Ticket Fare Calculator

This program reads age of the person and calculates the ticket fare based on following rules:

  • Normal ticket rates:
    • Mumbai - 600
    • Bengaluru - 450
    • Mangaluru - 300
    • Haveri - 350
  • For children below 15 it is half rate (50% of normal rate)
  • For senior citizens above 60 it is 60% of normal rate
